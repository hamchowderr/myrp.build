# GTA V Lore & Brand Naming

GTA V has a rich satirical universe with hundreds of fictional brands, businesses, and locations that parody real-world counterparts. When generating FiveM resources, all business names, brand names, and location names MUST fit the GTA universe.

## Naming Rules

1. **Always check for a canonical GTA V parody first** — if Rockstar already created a parody, USE IT
2. **Never use real-world brand names** — this breaks immersion and can cause legal issues on RP servers
3. **Match GTA's satirical tone** — names are tongue-in-cheek, often with double meanings or crude humor
4. **Consider the location theme** — Los Santos names have LA/Hollywood vibes; Sandy Shores is rural; Paleto Bay is small-town

## Creating Lore-Friendly Names

When no canonical parody exists, generate names that:

### Match the GTA Tone

- **Double entendres**: Names that sound normal but have a crude or humorous second meaning
- **Obvious parodies**: Slight name changes that are clearly referencing the original ("Bean Machine" for Starbucks)
- **Cultural satire**: Names that mock American consumer culture, corporate greed, or LA lifestyle
- **Portmanteaus**: Combining two words in unexpected ways

### Examples by Category

**Restaurants/Food:**

- Italian restaurant → "Mama's Meatball Palace" or "Tony's" (GTA has "Tony's Italian")
- Sushi → "Raw Deal Sushi" or "Bento Banzai"
- Mexican → "Taco Bomb" (canonical) or "El Cheapo's"

**Services:**

- Towing → "Hook, Line & Thinker Towing"
- Mechanic → "Los Santos Customs" (canonical) or "Fix-a-Wreck"
- Legal → "Shark & Associates" or "Dewey, Cheatem & Howe"

**Locations/Venues:**

- Nightclub → "Bahama Mamas" (canonical) or "Club Debauchery"
- Bar → "Tequi-la-la" (canonical) or "The Rusty Nail"
- Strip club → "Vanilla Unicorn" (canonical)

## Using Lore in Resources

When generating a resource (shop, dealership, job, etc.), always:

1. **Check if a canonical location/business exists** — use it if it does
2. **Name the resource with a lore-friendly name** — not "car-dealership" but "premium-deluxe-motorsport" (canonical) or a custom lore name
3. **Use GTA vehicle manufacturer names** — not "BMW M3" but "Übermacht Sentinel"
4. **Use GTA location names** — not "downtown" but "Legion Square", "Pillbox Hill", "Alta Street"
5. **Use GTA currency** — always just "$" with no real-world currency name
